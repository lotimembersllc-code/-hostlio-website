About Hostlio Pro

Hostlio Pro is an AI-powered hotel management platform built for independent hotels, boutique B&Bs, hostels, and small properties worldwide. We believe every independent hotelier deserves enterprise-level tools — without the enterprise-level price tag.

Built by Loti Members LLC, a California-based technology company, Hostlio Pro combines the power of artificial intelligence with deep hospitality expertise to automate guest communication, synchronize bookings across 10+ OTA channels, and give hotel owners their time back.

Our Mission

Independent hotels are the heart of travel. They offer authentic experiences that large chains simply can't replicate. Yet they're forced to compete with limited resources against properties with massive tech budgets.

Our mission is simple: level the playing field. By automating repetitive tasks and handling guest communications in 20+ languages, 24/7, we give independent hoteliers the freedom to focus on what they do best — creating unforgettable guest experiences.
